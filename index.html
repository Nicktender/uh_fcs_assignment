FCS Cooking Techniques

By Graeson

Bake

Cooking in an oven using dry heat. Example: bread.

Roast

Cooking with dry heat at high temperature. Example: roasted chicken.

Broil

Cooking under direct heat, similar to grilling. Example: broiled fish.

Sear

Browning the surface of food at high temperature to enhance flavor. Example: seared steak.

Saute

Cooking quickly in a small amount of oil or butter. Example: saut√©ed vegetables.

Pan-Fry

Cooking in a small amount of oil over moderate heat. Example: fried fish.

Deep-Fry

Cooking food in hot oil. Example: french fries.

Poach

Cooking food in gently heated liquid. Example: poached eggs.

Simmer

Cooking food gently just below boiling. Example: soup.

Boil

Cooking food in water at a high temperature. Example: boiling pasta.

Blanch

Quickly boiling and then cooling food. Example: blanching vegetables.

Steam

Cooking with steam from boiling water. Example: steamed vegetables.

Braise

Slow-cooking food in a small amount of liquid. Example: braised beef.

Grill

Cooking over direct heat, usually on a grill. Example: grilled steak.
